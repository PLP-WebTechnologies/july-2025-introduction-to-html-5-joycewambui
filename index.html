About This Page

Hello! I’m wambui joyce, and this is my webpage.

I built this page to learn the basics of HTML5 — like using semantic tags, adding headings, and writing content that’s easy to read for everyone (including search engines and screen readers).

It’s just a start, but I’m excited to keep learning web development and make even better pages in the future!

Why We Love Semantic HTML

Imagine writing a story where every chapter has a title, and each part fits in the right place. Semantic HTML works the same way: it helps readers (and browsers) know what’s important and where to look next.
